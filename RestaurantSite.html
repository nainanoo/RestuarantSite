Milky Way Creamery was founded by Dallas natives Naina and Shanil Noorani who spent many nights of their childhood looking at the stars through a telescope in their backyard. In 2019, they started Milky Way Creamistry, where the flavors and designs are inspired by planets, asteroids, moons, and stars in our galaxy. In addition to ice cream, we also serve space-themed pastries and drinks.
